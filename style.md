TYPOGRAPHY (THIS MATTERS A LOT)

Use font sizes, not colors, for hierarchy.

Example:

Hero text: 48–64px

Section titles: 24–32px

Body text: 16–18px

Muted text: lower opacity

Avoid:
❌ All-caps everywhere
❌ Fancy fonts
❌ Too many font weights


COLORS (LESS IS MORE)

Recommended palette:

Background: #f7f7f7 or #ffffff

Text: #111

Muted text: #777

Accent: soft blue or charcoal

No gradients. No neon.
